MongolianRice

Description

Mongolian rice is spicy dish with loads of different meats and veggies.It has a distinct taste with hints of wild garlic, while meat from the steppe areas is known for its rich wild onion flavor

Ingredients

  • cooked rice 170g
  • chicken 70g
  • onion 25g
  • carrot 25g
  • bell pepper 15g
  • pepper 1tsp
  • cabbage 25g
  • ginger 1tsp
  • garlic 1tsp
  • oyster sauce 4tsp
  • chilli paste 1tsp
  • 1 egg
  • oil 2tsp
  • sugar 1tsp
  • 1 sausage

Steps

  1. To a bowl, add the chopped chicken. Add ½ tsp. salt, ½ tsp. pepper and ½ tsp. chili powder and mix well.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp. oil in a pan and add the chicken and beef and shrimp, stir fry on high heat for about 5 minutes, or until cooked through and remove to a plate.
  3. To the same pan add 2 tbsp. oil and add minced garlic and ginger and stir fry for half a minute or until the ginger and garlic are soft. Then add the sliced onion and stir fry for 2 minutes or until the onion softens, on medium high heat.
  4. Add in the meat and mix well.
  5. Push the contents in the pan aside to make a circular empty space in the center and then add in the 2 eggs along with ¼ tsp. salt, ¼ tsp. pepper and ½ tsp. chili powder to that space. Then scramble the eggs by mixing the eggs as they cook and set to a semi firm consistency.
  6. Add the carrots, cabbage and green chili and stir fry for about a minute.
  7. Add, chili sauce, oyster sauce and soy sauce, ½ tsp. chili powder and stir fry for half a minute.
  8. Add the green onion, and stir fry for a minute while giving it a good mix.
  9. Then add in the chilled rice and combine until everything is mixed and thats it
